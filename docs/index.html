How do I get started?

To get started, click on the create food button and grow your population until you get around to 20 or so. Then, with the gains from farmers, start increasing your production and making buildings. This will open up the doors to culture & faith. Start purchasing technologies as well to make the msot of it, and keep exploring! Once your farmers are more optimized, you can begin diversifying your citizenry.
